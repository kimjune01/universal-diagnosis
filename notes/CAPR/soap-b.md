Capricor said deramiocel was progressively de-risked across snapshots, with each trial rewriting the program: after ALLSTAR failed in MI, the company pivoted to DMD, shifted from intracoronary to IV repeat dosing, increased dose, and moved from exploratory signals to a registrational Phase 3 design. **O**  
Temporal snapshots:

- `t1 ALLSTAR`: MI indication, intracoronary delivery, 25M cells, single dose, scar-reduction endpoint, failed for futility.
- `t2 HOPE-1`: DMD pivot begins. Same broad cell platform, but new disease context; safety/open-label signal generation.

- `t1 ALLSTAR`: Consolidate stack functional. The company read the failure correctly as wrong indication/route/endpoint, not “cell platform dead.”
- `t2 HOPE-1`: Early rewrite. This was the first substrate rewrite toward DMD, but still exploratory.
- `t3 HOPE-2`: Mixed snapshot. Cache stack showed biological signal, but the evidence package was too thin. Forward pass looked promising; regulatory handoff was still broken.
- `t4 HOPE-3`: Repaired system. The important fact is not just that the drug “worked,” but that Capricor kept the useful pieces constant (DMD, IV, repeat dosing, 150M cells) and rewrote the weak interfaces (sample size, endpoint architecture, placebo control, registrational packaging).

Natural Framework reading: the molecule is only half the pipe. The company is the consolidate stack. Capricor’s advantage was not perfection at `t3`; it was iterative repair from `t1 -> t4`. Shkreli diagnosed the cache stack in isolation. The retrospective truth is that the consolidate stack was functional and compounding.

**P**  
Retrospectively, the framework would have predicted:

- Do not anchor on the HOPE-2/BLA snapshot alone.
- Weight the temporal trajectory: ALLSTAR failure was absorbed, not repeated.
- Treat the July 2025 CRL as a broken handoff in evidence packaging, not decisive mechanism failure.
- Expect HOPE-3 to have a real chance of success because the company kept structurally rewriting the weak links rather than cosmetically defending the prior package.

In short: the framework would have been long the trajectory, not necessarily long the `t3` snapshot. That is why it beats the Shkreli short in retrospect.
