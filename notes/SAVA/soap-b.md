**S**  
Cassava Sciences (`SAVA`) was effectively the consolidate stack for simufilam, and the stack was corrupted at its base: the filamin-A/Alzheimer’s mechanism depended heavily on work from collaborator Hoau-Yan Wang, later indicted in June 2024 for allegedly fabricating preclinical data tied to NIH grants, while the SEC later alleged manipulated Phase 2 biomarker disclosures and extracted a $40 million settlement on September 26, 2024. Management spent 2021-2024 defending the science procedurally and rhetorically rather than re-underwriting it, even as the citizen-petition/data-integrity crisis persisted. By November 2024, Martin Shkreli publicly shorted SAVA and argued simufilam was mechanistically impossible; days later `RETHINK-ALZ` failed all endpoints and the stock collapsed about 86%, and `REFOCUS-ALZ` then failed all endpoints on March 25, 2025. This is a clean recurrence of the same trauma: a drug advanced on disputed and later legally tainted substrate failed when tested in large blinded Phase 3 trials.

**S**  
Cassava Sciences (`SAVA`) was effectively the consolidate stack for simufilam, and the stack was corrupted at its base: the filamin-A/Alzheimer’s mechanism depended heavily on work from collaborator Hoau-Yan Wang, later indicted in June 2024 for allegedly fabricating preclinical data tied to NIH grants, while the SEC later alleged manipulated Phase 2 biomarker disclosures and extracted a $40 million settlement on September 26, 2024. Management spent 2021-2024 defending the science procedurally and rhetorically rather than re-underwriting it, even as the citizen-petition/data-integrity crisis persisted. By November 2024, Martin Shkreli publicly shorted SAVA and argued simufilam was mechanistically impossible; days later `RETHINK-ALZ` failed all endpoints and the stock collapsed about 86%, and `REFOCUS-ALZ` then failed all endpoints on March 25, 2025. This is a clean recurrence of the same trauma: a drug advanced on disputed and later legally tainted substrate failed when tested in large blinded Phase 3 trials.

**O**  
2021-08-25: management responds to citizen-petition allegations by calling them false/misleading, showing broken `read_outcomes`.  
2021-08-30 to 2022-02-01: SPA agreement and FDA petition denial are used as procedural shields rather than scientific re-evaluation, so broken `read_outcomes` transitions into broken/stressed `batch_process`.  
2021-11-03 and 2021-11-18: `RETHINK-ALZ` and `REFOCUS-ALZ` launch without meaningful substrate rewrite, so `write_substrate` remains nominally functional but not corrective.  
2024-06-28: Wang indictment converts the earlier integrity concern into formal trauma confirmation; `batch_process` is now clearly broken.  
2024-07-17: CEO Remi Barbier and SVP Lindsay Burns resign, a partial governance repair but not a scientific repair.  
2024-09-26: SEC charges Cassava, Barbier, Burns, and Wang; Cassava settles for $40M, confirming manipulated Phase 2 biomarker representations and deepening the break between claimed signal and real substrate.  
2024-11-21: Shkreli publishes his short thesis, functioning as an external honest `read_outcomes` event.  
2024-11-25: `RETHINK-ALZ` fails both co-primary endpoints and all secondary/exploratory endpoints; company discontinues the ongoing `REFOCUS-ALZ` and OLE, making the transition from stressed system to outright collapse.  
2025-03-25: `REFOCUS-ALZ` also fails both co-primary endpoints at both doses and all other endpoints, confirming no hidden efficacy signal across roughly 2,000 Phase 3 patients.  
2025-2026: legal overhang is gradually cleared, company pivots and renames, but that is post-failure cleanup, not recovery of simufilam-in-Alzheimer’s.
